About Buddika Stores

Serving our community with dedication and quality products since 1985.

Our Story

Buddika Stores began with a simple mission: to provide the local community with easy access to high-quality daily essentials. What started as a small retail shop has grown into a trusted name for groceries, stationery, and newspapers.

We believe in the power of community. Every product on our shelves is selected with our customers' needs in mind. From the student looking for the perfect pen to the family shopping for weekly groceries, we are here to serve you.

Ideally positioned to serve you better, we continue to expand our range and improve our services, ensuring that Buddika Stores remains your preferred local partner.
