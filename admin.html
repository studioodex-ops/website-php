📖 How to Send WhatsApp Broadcast

  1. Create your message using the message builder on the left
  2. Copy the message using the "Copy Message" button
  3. Select customers and copy their numbers
  4. Open WhatsApp on your phone → Menu (⋮) → New Broadcast
  5. Add contacts using the copied numbers
  6. Paste your message and send!

💡 Tip: Customers must have your number saved to receive broadcast messages.
